What is Autism?

Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) is a neurodevelopmental condition that affects communication, social interaction, and behavior.

Early Signs

  • Limited eye contact
  • Delayed speech development
  • Repetitive behaviors
  • Difficulty in social interaction

Myths in India

  • Autism is caused by bad parenting
  • Autism is a disease
  • Early support can not improves quality of life

Support in India

India provides special education support, inclusive schools, and government disability benefits for children with autism.
